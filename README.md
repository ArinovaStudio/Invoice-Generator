<h3><b>Project Overview</b></h3>
<p><b>Arinvoice is a streamlined, professional invoicing solution built to eliminate the friction of getting paid. It allows users to generate, manage, and export high-quality PDF invoices in seconds, featuring integrated UPI QR codes for instant settlement. Unlike traditional accounting software, Arinvoice offers a premium, bento-style dashboard experience without the monthly fees or "Pro" paywalls.</b></p>

<hr />

<h3><b>Core Features</b></h3>
<ul>
  <li><b>Dynamic UPI QR Payments:</b> Every invoice includes a dynamic QR code locked to the total. Get paid directly into your bank account instantly.</li>
  <li><b>Client & Company CRM:</b> A premium dashboard to manage your clients, company profiles, GST details, and addresses in one place.</li>
  <li><b>Professional PDF Templates:</b> Choose from 12+ premium, modern layouts that make your brand look elite.</li>
  <li><b>Tax & GST Ready:</b> Out-of-the-box support for GST, CGST, SGST, IGST, and TDS calculations.</li>
  <li><b>Free Email Delivery:</b> Send invoices directly to clients via email with no SMTP setup and no hidden fees.</li>
  <li><b>Real-Time Alerts:</b> Know exactly when your client opens an invoice or completes a payment.</li>
  <li><b>Multi-Currency Support:</b> Built for global business, allowing you to bill international clients with ease.</li>
</ul>
